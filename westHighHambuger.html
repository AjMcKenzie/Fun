    News, Events, and Announcements

    FarmBot: Collaborative Garden Project

    Davenport West High School students are building a robot to be used next spring in gardens at Buffalo Elementary School.

    The “FarmBot” project is the brainchild of Buffalo Principal Heidi Gilliland as project-based learning that involves technology students at Davenport West,  building trade students from Mid-City, and grade school students in Buffalo.  Read More in the QC Times.

    What About the Boys?

    On November 17th, ten students from West High School attended the “What About the Boys?” Conference hosted by Bethany For Children & Families at Western Illinois University (QC Campus). These students participated in break-out sessions regarding the challenges and opportunities presented to young men today. The highlight of the day included the keynote address from community activist and Augustana graduate, Tayvian Johnson. Tayvian shared his “personal game plan” to win at life.

    West WINGs Girls Attend Fearlessly Girls Conference

     

    123►

    The Davenport West WINGs Girls and adviser Mrs. Iavarone attended the Fearlessly Girls conference! Several West girls bravely spoke in front of the audience of 7000 girls viewing the event both in person and online! The girls were really impressed to see a QC celebrity tennis player, Madison Keys, speak to bringing about a Kinder Girl climate both in person and online.
